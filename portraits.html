Photo Shoot Information

Location

The photo shoot can be in-studio, outside, or at your home. This is your choice. My personal feeling is that natural light (outside) is the best choice for beauty and natural skin tones. We will spend two hours together posing and shooting to capture impressive portraits of you.

Clothing

Plan on three different sets of clothes for the photo shoot. Choose outfits that you are comfortable wearing and reflect your personality. You can add depth and beauty to your portrait if you choose an outfit with texture, colors, layers and jewelry. You can enhance the look with a hat and/or accessories.

Makeup

We do recommend that women wear some makeup for the photo shoot. My philosophy is that “a little goes a long way”. Wear enough that you are comfortable with. Do not overdo it. This is your time to look beautiful and create gorgeous images for you, family and friends. We can hire a professional Hair and Makeup Artist upon your request for an additional fee.

Photography Style

We like to photograph in both color and black-and-white formats. In color images, there is a rich spectrum of various colors in your portrait that will “pop” with brilliance. However, black-and-white images appear timeless and have true value to them that make you want to keep the image forever. During the transformation to black-and-white, the colors from the portrait become subtle shades of gray, brilliant white, and deep black. It is reduced to pure form and tones, which creates a timeless image.

Process

Once the session photo shoot has been completed, you can expect a link within two weeks to a private online gallery where you can view the proofs. There will be approximately 40 proof images. Of these proofs, you will select the best 20 as the final images for printing.
